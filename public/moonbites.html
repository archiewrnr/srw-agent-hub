🔍 Sleep supplement category pattern scan

Cross-referenced against current top-spending Meta advertisers in the sleep/calm category (Ritual, AG1-adjacent gummy brands, Cymbiotika, Moon Juice) before picking the 6 templates below.

Pattern 1
Time-stamped pain point openers ("3:47am") consistently outperform generic "can't sleep?" hooks — specificity reads as authentic, not scripted.
Pattern 2
Lo-fi, handheld-feel UGC is beating studio-polished creative by a wide margin on cost-per-result — the algorithm and the audience both favour "real person" framing over an obvious ad.
Pattern 3
"Switcher" creative (melatonin-free, no-grogginess positioning) wins on intent — it targets people who already decided to buy a sleep aid and just need a reason to pick this one.
Pattern 4
Carousels structured as before/after (Day 1 vs Day 30) hold attention longer than single-image static, and they're the strongest format for retargeting site visitors who didn't convert.
