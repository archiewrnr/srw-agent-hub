What We're Actually Sourcing From

Honest note: no ad library — Meta's own or Atria's — exposes impressions, spend or conversions for anyone else's ads. That data is private to the ad account owner, full stop. What's real and checkable: how long an ad has stayed live (advertisers kill losers fast and keep funding winners), how many ads a brand is running in total, placement breadth, and public SimilarWeb traffic estimates for the brand's own site. The score on each card below is built from those three real signals — shown openly, not a black-box number.
